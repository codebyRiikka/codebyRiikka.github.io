About Me

In addition to programming, I have studied the fundamentals of ICT and basic embedded systems, which I also find fascinating. Now, I believe I have found a new direction for my career, and I am very determinedly pursuing it. In my free time, I enjoy various forms of exercise, especially long-distance running and cycling.
